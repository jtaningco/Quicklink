Need more help understanding what Quicklink does for you?

Here are some of the frequently asked questions about our platform.

General

What is Quicklink?

Quicklink is a platform that makes the buying and selling aspect of a transaction easier for both sides! We automate processes for the sellers, and we provide an all-in-one buying platform for the buyers.

What do I get from using Quicklink?

Quicklink will streamline the entire business transaction from order to payment, making it more efficient and easy to manage. We can help you grow your business by equipping you with analytical tools, automated order forms and tracking, and payment gateways that can provide you and your customers a smoother experience. Learn more about it here.

How can this help my customers?

Your customers get the best, inclusive experience by allowing your customers to pay with different methods, track their order, and provide them an easy-to-use menu and order form.

How much does it cost?

Using Quicklink on its standard plan is entirely free!

Who can use Quicklink?

For now, our platform is only optimized for online food sellers. However, expect us to expand to other types of products like clothing, stationaries, and more, as well as on-site physical restaurants soon. Stay tuned!

What payment methods can I use as a shop customer?

We are looking to accept payments through GCash, Visa, Mastercard, Bank of the Philippine Islands, and Banco de Oro.

What do I get from the early access program?

You'll get firsthand access to our product once it's available for use! As an early access customer, we keep you up-to-date with news about Quicklink and its features. You'll also be given an opportunity to help us build the platform!

How do I apply for early access?

By simply clicking on the "Join Early Access" button and filling out the form, you'll be invited to join our early access program! Just wait for an email from us, we'll get back to you in a few days!

About the Product

How does Quicklink automate order and financial processes?

Quicklink does this by combining all processes involved in a point-of-sale into one platform — from ordering to payment. Doing so automates the documentation of these data for a seller as well.

How much time does Quicklink save?

We can lessen the order time from more than 10 minutes to less than 2 minutes. Even better, there won't be a need to dedicate manpower to reply to or update your customers.

How does Quicklink make a shop customer's life easier?

We get rid of the long "message to order, wait for reply, get payment details, transfer through a banking app, take a screenshot, send for confirmation" process for customers! Besides this, customers can also have their details automatically filled out, such as name, address, and payment methods, given that they have a Quicklink Customer Account.

Do I need to be tech-savvy to use Quicklink?

Nope! Our designers are working to make sure that your use of Quicklink is as easy and accessible as possible. No need to code anything. We aim to provide the best experience for our users!
